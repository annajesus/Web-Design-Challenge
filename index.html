Summary: Latitude vs. X

The purpose of this project was to analyze how weather changes as you get closer to the equator. We utilized data from the OpenWeatherMap API to collect data for over 500 cities of varying distances from the equator.

Once the dataset was collected, Matplotlib was used to plot the various results of the realtionship of weather vs. latitude. The following relationships were analyzed: Latitude vs. Temperature, Latitude vs. Humidity, Latitude vs. Cloudiness, Latitude vs. Wind Speed.
